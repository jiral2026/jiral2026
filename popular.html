WELL-KNOWN GROUPS

Popular groups from each generation along with their biggest hits!

Get to know some of the most popular Kpop groups in each generations, the ones who served as a rolemodel for the next generations to come. The ones who set the standards, who made the "perfect recipe" to Kpop stardom, the ones who are considered to be the "OGs."

1st Generation

H.O.T. (aka Highfive Of Teenagers)

Korean Name: 에이치오티
Company: SM Entertainment
Debut Date: September 7, 1996
Disbandment Date: May, 2001
Members: Heejun, Woohyuk, Tony, Kangta, Jaewon

Their most popular song, "Candy," was apart of their debut album. It was described as a cheerful and bubblegum pop dance track, and upon its release, it was recognized nationwide in South Korea. It was even voted as the most popular song of 1996 in South Korea in a survey conducted in 2005 by MTV Korea. The group even won the "Best New Artist" award during the 11th Golden Disc Awards because of Candy.

H.O.T - Candy, HOT - 캔디, MBC Top Music 19961207
S.E.S. (aka Sea, Eugene, Shoo)

Korean Name: 에스이에스
Company: SM Entertainment
Debut Date: November 1, 1997
Disbandment Date: December, 2002
Members: Bada (Sea), Eugene, Shoo

One of their most popular songs was considered to be "('Cause) I'm Your Girl," which was a sing;e. It appeared on SM Entertainment's compilation album, SM Best Album 2. It lead to a release of another single called "Oh, My Love," which gained the number 2 spot on Inkigayo.

2nd Generation

TVXQ

Korean Name: 동방신기
Company: SM Entertainment
Debut Date: December 26, 2003
Disbandment Date: N/A
Members: U-Know Yunho, Max Changmin, Hero Jaejoong, Micky Yoochun, Xiah Junsu (Now only Yunho & Changmin)

The song was released on September 26, 2008, in their fourth studio album. Mirotic seems to be the (now duo)'s most popular song as it has been performed so many times and was covered by so many idols. Music critics truly loved the songs and was dubbed one of the best boy band songs of all time by Billboard and Rolling Stone.

TVXQ! 동방신기 '주문 - MIROTIC' MV
Girls' Generation (SNSD)

Korean Name: 소녀시대
Company: SM Entertainment
Debut Date: August 5, 2007
Disbandment Date: N/A
Members: Taeyeon, YoonA, Tiffany, Sunny, Hyoyeon, Sooyoung, Seohyun, Yuri, Jessica (Jessica has left)

"I Got a Boy" is considered to be the group's best song, according to many music critics. It has a unique rhythm and concept which made it popular both in South Korea and internationally. After opening with singsong raps then jumping from bubblegum to drum & bass in a such a quick moment, the tempo-shifting banger is a force of nature.

Girls' Generation 소녀시대 'I GOT A BOY' MV

3rd Generation

BTS (Bangtan Boys)

Korean Name: 방탄소년단
Company: Big Hit Music (Apart of Hybe)
Debut Date: June 13, 2013
Disbandment Date: N/A
Members: RM, Jin, Suga, J-Hope, Jimin, V, Jungkook.

"Dynamite" was their first full-English single and is now the most-streamed BTS song ever. It's an upbeat pop song that contains elements of funk, soul, and bubblegum pop. The song was released during the pandeimc and was intended to give hope and joy to those suffering due to Covid-19, it was meant to give them the mindset to appreciate the little things and realize how valuable life is.

BTS (방탄소년단) 'Dynamite' Official MV
Twice

Korean Name: 트와이스
Company: JYP Entertainment
Debut Date: October 20, 2015
Disbandment Date: N/A
Members: Jihyo, Nayeon, Jeongyeon, Momo, Sana, Mina, Dahyun, Chaeyoung, Tzuyu

"Cheer Up" is a song that combines multiple music genres which adds up to the genre, "Color Pop." The song is about expressing your frustration on a love interest, which was a relatable topic for many teens during that time and even now. The song topped the Gaon Digital Chart and is the best-performing single of 2016 in South Korea. It also won several awards including Song of the Year in two major music awards shows, Melon Music Awards and Mnet Asian Music Awards.

TWICE "CHEER UP" M/V

4th Generation

Stray Kids

Korean Name: 스트레이 키즈
Company: JYP Entertainment
Debut Date: March 25, 2018
Disbandment Date: N/A
Members: Bang Chan, Lee Know, Changbin, Hyunjin, Han, Felix, Seungmin, I.N

"God's Menu" was the main track of their first ever studio album, and was a hit that got them recognized internationally. Arguably one of Stray Kids' most popular tracks, “God's Menu” shockingly never got a music show win during their promotional period. With its unique concept and standout choreography, it's definitely surprising to hear that this track didn't snag a trophy.

Stray Kids "神메뉴(God's Menu)" M/V
Itzy

Korean Name: 있지
Company: JYP Entertainment
Debut Date: February 11, 2019
Disbandment Date: N/A
Members: Yeji, Lia, Ryujin, Chaeryeong, Yuna

"Dalla Dalla," which was Itzy's debut song, was an instant hit and scored one of the biggest Billboard debuts for a new K-pop act in years, it even peaked at number two on the World Digital Song Sales chart. The song brought instant success to the group, the music video being able to hit 100 million views in less than two months, which was already amazing for a rookie group.
