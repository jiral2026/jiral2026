ABOUT THE AUTHOR!

Hi, it's me the author and/or creator of this website! You probably already know, but I am Jaeda Iral. I made this website for many different reasons. One of the reasons why I made this website is because, totally shockingly, I am a Kpop fan myself! I enjoy listening to Kpop almost everyday, whether I'm at school, at home, in the mall, etc. It is just something that I have incorporated into my lifestyle and I think you should to.

Another one of the many reasons why I have made this website, was to make it an easy and accessible place for people (who want to become a Kpop stan) to find the motivation and I guess.. Information about what they're actually getting into. People think that Kpop is just a hobby but it can be a LIFESTYLE! Of course, not everyone has to be a hardcore Kpop stan, there are millions of just casual listeners out there, but I of course, am promoting for one to be a HARDCORE KPOP STAN! Sure, there are pros, there are cons, but putting all these factors aside, being an avid listenter and supporter of Kpop is just fun. It's like having another safe space, another hobby to submerge yourself with, another hobby to spend your time on. It may seem that Kpop is just a useless hobby or trend that can die down in the future, but in the present, is something that people use as an escapegoat from reality.

I hope that this website may or may not have converted you into a Kpop stan or even just a casual listener. I just wanted to express myself through this website, and show what I find enjoyable and fun in my life. I just wanted more people to find something that they can use as a distraction from their problems, a little hobby, or just something that gives them a smidge of joy. Thank you for visiting my website!
